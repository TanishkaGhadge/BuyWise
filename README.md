🛍️ BuyWise – Smart Shopping & Price Comparison Platform

BuyWise is a smart shopping platform designed to help users make better purchasing decisions through **real-time price comparison, intelligent recommendations, and a seamless shopping experience**.

It bridges the gap between users and retailers by providing a centralized system where users can explore, compare, and choose products efficiently.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* 🔍 **Smart Product Search** – Easily search and explore products
* 📊 **Price Comparison** – Compare prices across different sellers
* 🛒 **Cart & Checkout System** – Smooth and intuitive buying flow
* ❤️ **Favorites System** – Save products for later
* 🧠 **AI Shopping Assistant** – Helps users make smarter choices
* 🖼️ **Image-Based Search** – Find products using images
* 👤 **User Authentication** – Secure login & signup system
* 🏪 **Retailer Dashboard** – Manage products and listings
* 🛠️ **Admin Panel** – Full control over platform data
